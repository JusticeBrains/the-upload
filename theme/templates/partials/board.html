About The Academic Board

The Academic Board

The Academic Board is the governing body of the University that formulate and enchances the academic policies of the University, to regulate and approve the programme of studies and examinations held in the University

COMPOSITION OF THE ACADEMIC BOARD

  • The Vice Chancellor
  • The Pro Vice Chancellor
  • All Deans
  • All Heads of Academic Departments and Centres
  • Professors
  • Senior Members of Council who are not members of the Academic Board in any other capacity
  • One member not below the rank of Associate Professor elected from each Faculty by the Senior Members of the Faculty. Where there are no Senior Members of professorial status, a senior lecturer shall be elected
  • The Registrar shall be the Secretary to the Board
